# SURVIVAL MODE PROGRESSION DESIGN

## Overview
The goal is to create a balanced and engaging survival experience that scales gradually from easy to challenging, with distinct phases and rewards. Each stage should provide a clear progression path for the player, building their skills and rewards along the way.

## Phases and Timings
### Stage 1: AWAKENING (0-2 minuter)
- **Feel:** Calm, exploratory, "warming up"
- **Duration:** 2 minutes

### Stage 2: PRESSURE (2-5 minuter)
- **Feel:** First real challenge, learn to dodge
- **Duration:** 3 minutes

### Stage 3: VARIATION (5-8 minuter)
- **Feel:** Multiple threats, build crafting matters
- **Duration:** 3 minutes

### Stage 4: ESCALATION (8-12 minuter)
- **Feel:** Real test, multiple problems at once
- **Duration:** 4 minutes

### Stage 5+: ENDLESS CHAOS (12+ minuter)
- **Feel:** Full power fantasy, scaling forever
- **Duration:** Unbounded

---

## Detailed Breakdown

### Stage 1: AWAKENING
**Enemy Types:** Basic CHASER
**Count alive max:** 3-8
**Behavior:** Slow, predictable, easy to kill
**Spawn rate:** Every 3-5 seconds
**Boss:** NONE
**Buff cards:** NONE
**Stage end:** Survival time > 2 minutes OR 30 kills

### Stage 2: PRESSURE
**Enemy Types:** CHASER + RANGED
**Count alive max:** 8-15
**Behavior:** More aggressive, ranged shoots
**Spawn rate:** Every 2-3 seconds
**Boss:** Mini-boss at end (50% HP, easier)
**First buff card:** ~90 seconds in
**Stage end:** Mini-boss defeated

### Stage 3: VARIATION
**Enemy Types:** CHASER + RANGED + DASHER + ELITE
**Count alive max:** 15-25
**Behavior:** Mix of patterns, different threats
**Spawn rate:** Every 1.5-2 seconds
**Boss:** Real boss (normal difficulty)
**Buff cards:** Every 60 seconds
**Stage end:** Boss defeated

### Stage 4: ESCALATION
**Enemy Types:** CHASER + RANGED + DASHER + ELITE + TANK + SWARM groups
**Count alive max:** 20-30
**Behavior:** Complex AI, harder to track
**Spawn rate:** Every 1-1.5 seconds
**Boss:** Hard boss with mechanics
**Buff cards:** Every 50 seconds (faster pace)
**Stage end:** Boss defeated

### Stage 5+: ENDLESS CHAOS
**Enemy Types:** All unlocked types
**Count alive max:** 30-40
**Behavior:** Full chaos
**Spawn rate:** Every 0.5-1 seconds
**Boss:** New boss every 2 minutes
**Buff cards:** Every 45 seconds
**Stage end:** Endless until player dies

## Visual/Audio Cues per Stage
### Stage 1: AWAKENING
- **Background Color Shift:** Soft blue with subtle stars
- **Music:** Calm ambient track
- **Text Overlay:** "STAGE 1: AWAKENING"
- **HUD:** Basic HUD showing survival time and kills

### Stage 2: PRESSURE
- **Background Color Shift:** Darker blue with more cosmic dust
- **Music:** Intensifying ambient track
- **Text Overlay:** "STAGE 2: PRESSURE"
- **HUD:** Advanced HUD showing survival time, kills, and buff card countdown

### Stage 3: VARIATION
- **Background Color Shift:** Deep purple with swirling particles
- **Music:** Energetic track with dynamic beats
- **Text Overlay:** "STAGE 3: VARIATION"
- **HUD:** Comprehensive HUD showing survival time, kills, boss health bar, and buff card countdown

### Stage 4: ESCALATION
- **Background Color Shift:** Dark red with heavy particle effects
- **Music:** Intense epic track with dramatic crescendos
- **Text Overlay:** "STAGE 4: ESCALATION"
- **HUD:** Advanced HUD showing survival time, kills, boss health bar, and buff card countdown

### Stage 5+: ENDLESS CHAOS
- **Background Color Shift:** Black with occasional glowing particles
- **Music:** Chaotic track with rapid rhythms
- **Text Overlay:** "STAGE 5+: ENDLESS CHAOS"
- **HUD:** Advanced HUD showing survival time, kills, and buff card countdown
